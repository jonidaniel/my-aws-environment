Mental disorders are very real and mental well-being isn't self-evident among young people. Affective disorders, anxiety disorders, eating disorders, psychosis... Even though life's hard sometimes, try to remember this: you'll make it through anything. And while waiting for salvation, accelerate yourself and launch Mental Run!
Mental Run is a 2D top-down running game for Android platforms where you steer a teen through daily life choices over a period of a hard school day. Collect things beneficial for your health on the way and earn points. Hone your coordination and reaction speed to the extreme and earn your place among the best!
